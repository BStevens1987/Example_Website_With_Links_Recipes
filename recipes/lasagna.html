Lasagna Recipe

Introduction

Oatmeal is delicious. What are you waiting for? Let's figure out how to make some!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles: Capello's Lasagna Sheets
  • Tomato sauce:Any kind
  • Meat: Italian sausage.
  • Cheese: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta.
  • Egg: An egg to bind the cheese mixture.

Steps

  1. Place pasta into pot and boil.
  2. Cook the sausage and add it to the tomato sauce.
  3. Combine the cheese and egg into a bowl and mix.
  4. Layer the cheese and meat sauce with the noodles.
  5. Bake.

French Bread Pizza Recipe

Introduction

French bread pizza is delicious. What are you waiting for? Let's figure out how to make some!

Ingredients

  • Pizza sauce: Rao's Homemade Pizza Sauce.
  • French bread: Any kind.
  • Cheese: Mozzarella.
  • Optional toppings: Sausage, pepperoni, thyme, and whatever else you want to add!

Steps

  1. Cut loaves in half and place on baking sheet.
  2. Disperse pizza sauce over bread and layer toppings on it.
  3. Bake. Slice it up and eat it!

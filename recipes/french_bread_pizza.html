World-Famous French Bread Pizza Recipe

Introduction

Going into this with recipe testing I was thinking it would more so be a recipe the kids would love – but it turns out that equally as well adults will absolutely love this French bread pizza too!

Ingredients

  • French bread: Just one loaf is all that you need! This saves you SO much time because you don’t have to make a pizza crust!
  • Pizza sauce: While you can choose your favorite pizza sauce, I recommend Prego Pizza Sauce Pizzeria Style.
  • Mozarrella Cheese: You can’t have a delicious pizza without cheese!
  • Optional toppings: Pepperoni, olives, tomatoes, and basil.

Steps

  1. Prep and slice: Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Slice the loaf of bread in half lengthwise. Lay the bread on a baking sheet.
  2. Add sauce, cheese and toppings: Spread evenly with the pizza sauce, top with cheese and favorite toppings.
  3. Bake and serve: Bake for 12-15 minutes or until heated through and cheese is melted. Slice and serve.

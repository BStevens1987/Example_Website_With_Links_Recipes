French Bread Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, vegetables, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • French bread: One loaf.
  • Pizza sauce: Rao's Homemade Pizza Sauce.
  • Mozarrella Cheese: Any kind.
  • Optional toppings: Pepperoni, olives, tomatoes, and basil.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Slice the loaf of bread in half lengthwise. Lay the bread on a baking sheet.
  2. Spread evenly with the pizza sauce, top with cheese and favorite toppings.
  3. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until heated through and cheese is melted. Slice and serve.

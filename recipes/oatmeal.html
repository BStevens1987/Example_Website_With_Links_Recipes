Oatmeal Recipe

Introduction

Oatmeal is delicious. What are you waiting for? Let's figure out how to make some!

Ingredients

  • Oats: Quaker Oats Old Fashioned.
  • Milk or water: any kind.
  • Banana, blueberries and nuts: any kind.
  • Additional flavors: cinnamon.

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a pot.
  2. Boil the ingredients for about 5 minutes.
  3. Take it off the heat and pour it into a bowl. You can eat it once it's cool enough.

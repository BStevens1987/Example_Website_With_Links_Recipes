Oatmeal Recipe

Oatmeal

I became obsessed with oatmeal in college (hello 2008) and havenâ€™t looked back since. While cooking oatmeal in the microwave is quick and easy, this stovetop oatmeal recipe is my go-to when I want a perfectly sweetened, fluffy bowl of oats.

The rundown: Simply combine oats, milk, banana, blueberries, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt on the stovetop. Bring to a boil and then turn down to low and let simmer for 3-5 minutes or until thickened.

Ingredients

  • Oats: we used and recommend Quaker Oats Old Fashioned, but you can also use any other rolled or quick-cooking oats.
  • Almond milk: feel free to use any kind of milk.
  • Banana and blueberries: we used banana and blueberries as all-natural sweeteners.
  • Flavors: we added a little vanilla, cinnamon, and salt

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients into a small saucepan and turn heat to medium/high.
  2. Bring to a boil. Then, turn the heat down to low/medium and continually stir for around 3-5 minutes as the oatmeal cooks and thickens.
  3. Once oatmeal is at the desired consistency, remove from heat, and serve immediately.
